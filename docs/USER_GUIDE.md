## Getting Started

### 1. Access the Training Platform
Visit the training dashboard at:
```
http://localhost:3000/dashboard/training
```

### 2. Browse Available Modules
You'll see a grid of training modules, each displaying:
- **Title** - Name of the training module
- **Difficulty Badge** - Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
- **Duration** - Estimated time to complete
- **Category** - Topic area (Email Security, Incident Response, etc.)
- **Steps** - Number of interactive steps
- **Points** - Total points available
- **Prerequisites** - Required prior modules (if any)

## Taking Training

### Starting a Module

1. Click the **"Start Training"** button on any module
2. The training player will load with the first step
3. You'll see:
   - Progress bar showing your completion percentage
   - Current score
   - Step counter
   - Training content

### Understanding Step Types

#### üìö INSTRUCTION Steps
- **Purpose:** Provide information and context
- **Action:** Read the content and click "Continue"
- **Points:** Usually 0 (informational only)

#### ‚ùì QUESTION Steps
- **Purpose:** Test your knowledge
- **Action:** Select or enter your answer and click "Submit"
- **Points:** Typically 10-15 points per question

**Question Types:**
- **Multiple Choice:** Click one of the provided options
- **True/False:** Click True or False button
- **Short Answer:** Type your answer in the text box

#### üìù TASK Steps
- **Purpose:** Apply your knowledge
- **Action:** Write a detailed response
- **Points:** Usually 15 points

#### üéØ ASSESSMENT Steps
- **Purpose:** Summarize what you learned
- **Action:** Review and click "Continue"
- **Points:** Usually 5 points

### Answering Questions

1. **Read Carefully** - Make sure you understand what's being asked
2. **Select Your Answer** - Click your choice or type your response
3. **Submit** - Click the "Submit Answer" button
4. **Review Feedback** - Read the explanation provided

### Understanding Feedback

After submitting an answer, you'll see:
- ‚úÖ **Green** = Correct answer
- ‚ùå **Red** = Incorrect answer
- **Explanation** - Why the answer is correct/incorrect
- **Points Earned** - How many points you received
- **Continue Button** - Move to the next step

## Progress Tracking

### During Training

**Progress Bar:**
- Shows your completion percentage
- Updates in real-time as you complete steps

**Score Display:**
- Located in the progress card
- Shows total points earned so far

**Step Counter:**
- Shows current step number
- Total steps in the module

### Completing a Module

After the last step, you'll see your **Results Screen** with:

#### Score Display
- Large percentage showing your final score
- Pass/Fail indication (70% required to pass)

#### Statistics
- **Points Earned** - Your total vs. maximum available
- **Steps Completed** - Should be all steps

#### Performance Feedback
- Personalized messages based on your score
- Encouragement and next steps

#### Actions
- **Back to Training** - Return to module list
- **Retake Module** - Try again for a better score

---

## Scoring System

### How Points Work
- Each question/task has a point value
- Correct answers earn full points
- Incorrect answers earn 0 points
- Instruction steps usually give 0 points

### Passing Grade
- **70% or higher** - Pass
- **Below 70%** - Should retake module

### Score Ranges
- **90-100%** - Excellent! Expert level understanding
- **80-89%** - Great! Strong grasp of concepts  
- **70-79%** - Good! Passing with room for improvement
- **Below 70%** - Review material and try again

## Common Questions

### Can I pause during training?
Currently, sessions must be completed in one sitting. Set aside enough time before starting.

### What happens if I get disconnected?
Your session will be lost. You'll need to start the module again from the beginning.

### Can I retake modules?
Yes! Click "Retake Module" on the results screen or start the module again from the dashboard.

### How many times can I retake?
Unlimited! Keep practicing until you achieve the score you want.

### Do I get a certificate?
Currently certificates are not available, but your scores are tracked.

### Can I see correct answers?
The explanation after each question shows you the correct approach and why.

## Troubleshooting

### Training won't load
1. Check that both servers are running
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser

### Can't submit answer
1. Make sure you've selected/entered an answer
2. Check that the button isn't disabled
3. Refresh and try again

### Feedback not showing
1. Wait a moment for the response
2. Check your internet connection
3. Refresh the page if stuck

### Lost progress
Unfortunately, progress is not saved if you leave the page. Start over when you have time to complete the full module.
